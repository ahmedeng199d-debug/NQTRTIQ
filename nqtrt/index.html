Dashboard — Your Command Center
The first thing you see when entering NQTRT. All critical metrics rendered in real time from your logged trades.
📊
Top Stats Bar
The four chips across the top show Monthly P&L, Profit Factor, Win Rate, and Avg Win / Loss — all computed from closed trades only. These update instantly as you log trades.
📅
Trading Calendar
Each day cell shows net P&L and small colored dots per trade. Green background = net winning day, Red background = net losing day, Gold = breakeven. Click any day to see all trades for that session. Use the ‹ › arrows to navigate months.
📈
Equity Curve & P&L Chart
The bottom-left shows your cumulative equity curve (green when profitable, red when in drawdown) and a daily P&L bar chart for the last 14 days. These update in real time.
🏆
NQTRT Score
A composite 0–100 score based on your Win Rate (30pts), Profit Factor (20pts), Drawdown control (30pts), and Trade Volume (20pts). Use the dropdown to compare scores across accounts.
💹
Market Prices & Recent Trades
The right column shows live market references (EUR/USD, XAU/USD, BTC, etc.) and your 6 most recent logged trades with quick P&L and direction view.
WIN DAYNet positive P&L for that calendar day — green background on the cell
LOSS DAYNet negative P&L — red background, avoid stacking these consecutively
BREAKEVENZero net P&L for the day — gold/amber tint on the cell
TODAYCurrent day is highlighted with a red circle around the date number
➕
Logging a Trade — Full Workflow
The most important action in your journal. Log every trade — wins, losses, and breakevens. Discipline in logging = clarity in analysis.
📋
Trade Details — Core Fields
Fill in Date, Time, Pair/Symbol (e.g. EUR/USD, BTC/USD, NQ1!), Market (Forex or Crypto), and Session (London, NY, Asia). These fields drive your session-level analytics.
🎯
Direction & Timeframe
Click ▲ BUY or ▼ SELL for direction. Select your execution timeframe (M1 through W1). These are tracked separately so you can filter by direction bias over time.
🔮
ICT Concepts (Important)
Select every ICT concept that was part of your setup — Order Block, FVG, BOS, CHoCH, Liquidity Sweep, etc. NT AI uses this data to analyze which concepts produce your best results. Be honest and specific.
💰
P&L, R:R & Strategy
Enter your actual P&L in dollars (use negative for losses, e.g. -250). Enter R:R as 1:2.5. Select or create your strategy from the chip picker. Confidence 1–10 helps track your conviction accuracy.
🧠
Psychology & Notes
Always log your emotional state: Calm, Confident, Anxious, FOMO, Revenge, Tired, Overconfident. Write pre-trade notes (why you entered) and post-trade review (what happened). NT AI reads these for behavioral patterns.
📎
Attachments & Screenshots
Upload chart screenshots, PDFs, or paste TradingView links. Files are stored in base64 inside localStorage (max 10MB each). Click the upload zone or drag files directly onto it.
⚠️
Status matters: Set status to Win, Loss, or Breakeven after closing the trade. Trades marked Open are excluded from all P&L calculations and analytics.
≡
Trade Log — Full Analysis Table
Every trade in a filterable, sortable table. Your complete performance database at a glance.
📊
Performance Stats Strip
Six stat cards at the top show Today's P&L, Monthly P&L, Total P&L, Win Rate, Profit Factor, and Drawdown. These compute only from closed trades.
🚨
Risk Limit Progress Bars
Two bars show your Daily Limit and Total Limit usage. Green = safe, Gold = caution (80% reached), Red = limit hit — stop trading. Limits are set per account in the Accounts section. A warning banner appears at the top when you're close.
🔍
Filter Pills
Use the filter pills to show: All · Today · Wins · Losses · Open · Forex · Crypto. The count updates instantly.
🗑
Deleting Trades
Click the ✕ button on the far right of any row to permanently delete a trade. This action cannot be undone. All stats recalculate immediately after deletion.
🟢Profit Factor ≥ 1.5
Target. Means your winners outweigh losers by 1.5×. Above 2.0 = excellent edge.
🎯Win Rate ≥ 50%
With good R:R you can be profitable below 50%. Track both together, not separately.
📉R:R ≥ 1:1.5
Minimum acceptable reward-to-risk. Below 1:1 means you need 60%+ win rate just to break even.
🔴Drawdown Control
Never exceed your daily or total limit. Stop trading when the bars hit red — no exceptions.
📓
Notebook — Your Trading Journal
A private journal with tag-based organization. Link thoughts to trade dates. Track your mindset evolution over time.
✎
Creating a New Entry
Click New Entry to create a blank note. Add a title, write in the text area (markdown-like), and use #tags in the tags field (e.g. #FOMO #RevengeTrade #ASetup #London). Notes auto-save every 30 seconds.
💰
P&L Linkage
If you create a note on a date where you have closed trades, the net P&L for that day appears in the footer — green for profit, red for loss. This connects your thoughts to your performance automatically.
🔍
Search & Tags
Use the search bar to filter notes by title, body text, or tags. Add consistent tags like #FOMO, #RuleBreak, #GoodTrade to build a searchable behavior database over time.
⌨️
Keyboard Shortcut
Press Ctrl + S while in the Notebook to manually save the current entry. Entries are also auto-saved every 30 seconds when the notebook tab is open.
💡
Best practice: Write a brief note every trading day — even on days you don't trade. Document your market bias, session observations, and emotional state. Over 30+ days this becomes your most valuable edge asset.
🤖
NT AI — Your Trade-Aware Coach
Powered by Claude (Anthropic). Unlike generic AI, NT AI reads your actual trade history, ICT concept usage, psychology patterns, and real P&L data.
⚙️
Setup — API Key
Go to Settings ⚙ (click your profile chip or the settings button). Enter your Anthropic API key (sk-ant-...). The key is stored only in your browser's localStorage — it never leaves your device. Get your key at console.anthropic.com.
💬
What to Ask NT AI
NT AI knows your full trade history. Ask specific questions: "Why do I keep losing on Fridays?" · "Which ICT concept has my highest win rate?" · "When do I break my risk rules?" · "Compare my London vs NY session performance."
🔴
Floating NT AI Button
The red NT button in the bottom-right corner opens a quick-access chat panel with live stats. It's available on every page. Use it for quick pattern checks without leaving your current workflow.
🔒
Backend-Ready Architecture
For production security, the AI is designed to call POST /api/ai — your own backend proxy that holds the API key server-side. The frontend sends { message, context } and receives { reply }. Zero key exposure.
🎯
Pro tip: Log at least 20+ trades before asking NT AI for pattern analysis. The more data it has, the more specific and actionable its feedback becomes. Use the preset questions in the full AI page for guided analysis.
◑
Accounts & Settings
Manage multiple trading accounts with separate capital, risk limits, and trade histories. Switch instantly between profiles.
➕
Creating a Profile
Click New Profile and enter: Account name (e.g. Prop Firm - FTMO), Capital in USD, Daily Loss Limit % (e.g. 5 = 5%), and Total Loss Limit % (e.g. 10 = 10%). Each profile has completely separate trade data.
🔄
Switching Profiles
Click your profile chip in the bottom-left sidebar or the Settings button in the top-right. Select any profile to switch. The ACTIVE badge shows the current account. All dashboard stats, calendar, and logs update instantly.
⚠️
Risk Limits (Critical)
Daily Limit: Maximum loss allowed per day as % of capital. When hit — stop trading, no exceptions. Total Limit: Maximum cumulative drawdown. If you hit this, the account is effectively blown. NQTRT shows red warnings when you approach or exceed these thresholds.
🌐
Language & Theme
Click عربي in the sidebar to switch to Arabic (RTL layout). Click the moon/sun icon to toggle Light/Dark mode. All preferences are saved automatically and persist across sessions.
